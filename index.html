The most searched countries in March

Tokyo Japan

Visit the historic Meiji Shrine, an oasis of tranquility in the heart of Tokyo....Experience the bustling Shibuya Crossing, the busiest pedestrian crossing in the world. Enjoy the breathtaking cherry blossoms in Ueno Park during spring. Shop for cutting-edge technology and anime merchandise in Akihabara, Tokyo's tech hub. Savor authentic sushi, ramen, and other Japanese delicacies.

Paris France

Explore the Eiffel Tower, one of the most famous structures in the world....Discover the Louvre Museum, home to the Mona Lisa and countless other masterpieces. Enjoy the Seine River cruises, which offer a scenic view of Parisian architecture and landmarks. Indulge in French cuisine at world-class restaurants and street-side cafés. Stroll through Montmartre, a historic district famous for its art, cobbled streets, and the Sacré-Cœur Basilica.

Sydney Australia

Explore the iconic Sydney Opera House, a masterpiece of modern architecture and a hub for cultural performances....Relax at Bondi Beach, renowned for its golden sands and surfing culture. Take a scenic ferry ride to Manly for stunning views of the harbor and coastline. Visit Taronga Zoo to see Australian wildlife, including kangaroos and koalas. Embark on a day trip to the Blue Mountains, known for its dramatic cliffs, eucalyptus forests, and waterfalls.
